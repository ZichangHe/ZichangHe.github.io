About me

I am a first-year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Zheng Zhang. I received the B.Eng. degree in Detection, Gudiance and Control Technology from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU), Xi'an, China in 2018 under the supervision of Prof. Yong Deng and Prof. Wen Jiang. During my undergraduate research, I was a research intern at the Hong Kong Polytechnic Unviersity (PolyU) under the supervisor of Prof. Felix Chan.
