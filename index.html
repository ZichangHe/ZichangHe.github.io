About Me

I am a fourth-year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Zheng Zhang. I am widely working on Uncertainty Quantification and Tensor related topics with applications in Design Automation, Machine Learning, and Quantum Computing. Specific topics include

 

  • Uncertainty quantification and robustness of machine learning algorithms

  • General tensor learning and sampling algorithms

  • Simulation and uncertainty quantification of classical and quantum circuits

  • Uncertainty- and risk-aware engineering design of classical and quantum circuits
