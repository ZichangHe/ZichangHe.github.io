About Me

I am a researcher in Global Technology Applied Research at JPMorganChase. Prior to that, I received my PhD degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) in 2023. My research primarily focuses on the interdisciplinary topics of design automation and quantum computing.

 

  • Quantum algorithms and applications

  • Design automation of quantum computing

  • Uncertainty quantification and uncertainty-aware optimization
