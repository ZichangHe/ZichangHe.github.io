About Me

I am a third-year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Zheng Zhang. I am widely working on Uncertainty Quantification and Tensor related topics with applications on Design Automation, Machine Learning, and Quantum Computing. Specific topics include

  • Uncertainty quantification and robustness of machine learning algorithms

  • General tensor learning and sampling algorithms

  • Uncertainty and risk-aware engineering design of classical and quantum circuits

  • Simulation of classical and quantum circuits
