About me

I am a third-year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering under the supervision of Prof. Zheng Zhang. I am widely working on uncertainty quantification and tensor related topics with applications on design automation, machine learning, and control systems.

Before joining UCSB, I received the B.Eng. degree in Detection, Gudiance and Control Technology from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NWPU), Xi'an, China under the supervision of Prof. Yong Deng and Prof. Wen Jiang in 2018. I was a research intern at the Hong Kong Polytechnic Unviersity (PolyU) under the supervision of Prof. Felix Chan in the summer of 2017.
