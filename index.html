About Me

I am a researcher in Global Technology Applied Research at JPMorgan Chase. Prior to that, I received my PhD degree in ECE from the University of California, Santa Barbara (UCSB) in 2023 under the supervision of Prof. Zheng Zhang. My research primarily focuses on the interdisciplinary topics of design automation and quantum computing.

 

  • Quantum algorithms and applications

  • Design automation of quantum computing

  • Uncertainty quantification and uncertainty-aware optimization of classical circuits
